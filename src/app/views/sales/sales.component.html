Creating beautiful show horses has been a primary focus of the TLF program for over a decade. We can proudly say that some very successful show horses in Europe can claim a TLF origin. Occasionally we do offer some horses for sale. As we continue to breed toward the next generation unfortunately we cannot keep them all. From time to time a broodmare, stallion or young colt/gelding will come up for sale. Please contact us with any questions, we are happy to help you learn about the American mininature horse.

If you would like information on any of our horses please contact us.
