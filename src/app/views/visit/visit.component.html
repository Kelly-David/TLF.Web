Turra Lodge Farm & Gardens

Turra Lodge Farm and Gardens are now open to the public. Visit with the animals and explore our various lakeside gardens. 'The Stables' at Turra Lodge Farm is our new on-site café serving a variety of treats and refreshments, including barista coffee, ice-cream, soft drinks and snacks.

The Gardens

Over the past 20 years the Kelly Family have developed an extensive array of plants, trees and shrubbery in one of the most unique landscapes you will ever see. What once was an abandoned opencast coal mine has been transformed into a sanctuary for flora, fauna and visitors alike. In 2021, the gardens at Turra Lodge Farm where featured on the RTE series 'Ireland's Garden Heroes' with the Kelly Family being named one of Ireland's Garden Heroes. The farm and gardens were also featured on 'Beidh Mé Ar Ais' on TG4.

The Animals

Turra Lodge Farm is home to a variety of animals. Visitors will of course get to experience our American miniature horses but also see the several other species of rare breeds that live on the farm including: Pygmy Goats, Pot Bellied Pigs, Soay Sheep, American Silky Chickens, Bantam Chickens and Donkeys.
