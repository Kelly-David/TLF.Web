Our Farm

Turra Lodge Farm is a family owned and operated American miniature horse breeding program located in the south east of Ireland. The Kelly Family have owned and bred miniature horses since 2005. The farm is home to a small group of AMHA registered miniature horses, some of which are also registered with the AMHR or the BMHS. Each Spring brings the arrival of new foals. Our hope is that these individuals will become successful halter horses before contributing to our breeding program. Since the farm's inception we have enjoyed presenting our horses in Ireland, the United Kingdom and Europe.

Our aim at TLF is to breed beautiful American Miniature Horses with the most perfect conformation, attitude and athleticism for the showring, and we could not have found a more pleasurable pastime. We have carefully established a small herd of American Miniature Horses possessing extreme type, refinement and movement. We invite you to browse through our website and please feel free to contact us if you would like further information on any of our American miniature horses.

Showing is the farm's main activity. During the summer we enjoy presenting our horses at AMHA/AMR and ASPC sanctioned shows throughout Europe. We love to show horses we have bred so each year we will breed a small group of foals. It is our hope that these individuals will become accomplished halter horses before contributing to the gene pool. The focus of our breeding program is to breed beautiful and true to type American Miniature Horses. Halter competitions are where we can assess the quality of our breeding program so we aim to breed horses that are competitive at an international level.

Occasionally we do offer some horses for sale. As we continue to breed toward the next generation unfortunately we cannot keep them all. From time to time a broodmare, stallion or young colt/gelding will come up for sale. Please contact us with any questions, we are happy to help you learn about the American mininature horse.
