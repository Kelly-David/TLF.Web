Showing

Showing is the farm's main activity. During the summer we enjoy presenting our horses at AMHA, AMHR and ASPC sanctioned shows throughout Europe. We love to show horses we have bred so each year we will breed a small group of foals. It is our hope that these individuals will become accomplished halter horses before contributing to the gene pool. The focus of our breeding program is to breed beautiful and true to type American Miniature Horses. Halter competitions are where we can assess the quality of our breeding program so we aim to breed horses that are competitive at an international level.
