Welcome to Turra Lodge Farm

Turra Lodge Farm is a family owned and operated American miniature horse breeding program located in the south east of Ireland. The Kelly Family have owned and bred miniature horses since 2005. The farm is home to a small group of AMHA and AMHR registered miniature horses, some of which are also registered with the ASPC or the BMHS. Each Spring brings the arrival of new foals. Our hope is that these individuals will become successful halter horses before contributing to our breeding program. Since the farm's inception we have enjoyed presenting our horses in Ireland, the United Kingdom and Europe.
